The Greatest Empires of All Time

British Empire

The British Empire was the largest colonial empire in the history of mankind, with colonies on every continent.

Mongol Empire

The state founded by Temujin in 1206 included the largest contiguous territory in world history, from the Danube to the Sea of ​​Japan and from Novgorod to Cambodia.

Spanish Empire

In the period from 1580 to 1640, it included Portugal with its own colonial empire. In the first quarter of the 19th century, most of the Spanish colonies in Latin America achieved independence. The final liquidation of the empire took place in the middle of the 20th century.

Qing Empire

The last dynasty of monarchical China. In less than 30 years, all of China and part of Central Asia fell under her rule.

Second French Colonial Empire

In the 19th century, France took an active part in the struggle of the major powers to complete the division of the world.

Portuguese Empire

In 1640, after the rupture of the Iberian Union with Spain, the independence of Portugal was restored. In 1975, the Portuguese colonial empire ceased to exist.
